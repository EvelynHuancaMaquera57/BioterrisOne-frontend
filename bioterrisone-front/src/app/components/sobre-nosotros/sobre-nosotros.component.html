About Us

Mission

Our mission is to transform NASA satellite data into actionable insights for environmental health and risk assessment at the district level. We bridge the gap between complex scientific data and practical application.

By making environmental risk data accessible and understandable, we empower communities to make informed decisions about their health and environment.

Vision

To create a world where environmental data is accessible and understandable for everyone, empowering communities to make informed decisions about their health and environment.

We envision a future where every community has the tools to understand and mitigate environmental risks effectively.

The Problem We Address

Environmental risks and their impact on public health are often invisible and poorly understood. Communities lack access to clear, scientific data about environmental threats in their specific areas.

This information gap prevents effective planning, policy-making, and individual decision-making regarding environmental health risks.

Our Solution

BioterrisOne converts complex NASA satellite data into intuitive visualizations and clear information about environmental risks and their health impacts, making scientific data accessible to all.

Our platform provides district-level insights that help urban planners, researchers, and citizens understand and address environmental health challenges.

Why BioterrisOne

We bridge the gap between scientific data and practical application, providing tools that help urban planners, researchers, and citizens understand and address environmental health challenges.

Our unique approach combines NASA satellite data with local health impact analysis, creating a comprehensive environmental risk assessment tool.
